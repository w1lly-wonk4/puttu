Why Our Puttu Premix?

Light & Healthy

Authentic Taste

How It Works

  1. Measure the premix and add water as per instructions on the pack.
  2. Fill your puttu maker or steamer and cook for 5–10 minutes.
  3. Serve hot with curry, stew, or banana and sugar/jaggery.
